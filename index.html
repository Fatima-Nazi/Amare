About Amare Perfume

Midnight Alpha

Midnight Alpha is the signature perfume of our collection, designed for bold and sophisticated personalities. With its deep and captivating aroma, it offers an impressive 6–7 hours of long-lasting freshness, making every moment unforgettable.

Crafted with a perfect blend of rich and elegant notes, this fragrance creates a powerful presence wherever you go. Its smooth and refined scent reflects confidence, style, and timeless charm, making Midnight Alpha the perfect choice for those who want to stand out with elegance.

Subtle hints of exotic woods and delicate florals intertwine to awaken the senses, leaving a trail of allure and sophistication. Every spray tells a story of luxury, passion, and individuality—because true elegance is remembered long after you leave the room.
