Our Collection

At Amare Perfume, we believe that a fragrance is an invisible part of your personal style. Our diverse range of perfumes and attars is designed to cater to every mood, occasion, and personality.

Our Perfumes

Our perfume collection ranges from fresh aquatic notes perfect for the daytime to rich, woody aromas suited for the evening. We ensure high concentration levels in every bottle, guaranteeing that your scent stays with you throughout the day. Each fragrance is a blend of top, heart, and base notes that evolve beautifully over time.

Pure Attars

For those who prefer traditional luxury, our attars (ittars) are alcohol-free, pure oil-based fragrances derived from natural sources. These highly concentrated scents are gentle on the skin and offer a profound depth that synthetic perfumes cannot match. Just a single drop is enough to leave a lasting impression for hours.
